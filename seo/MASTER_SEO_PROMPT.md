## CORE SEO PHILOSOPHY

### 1. Traditional SEO (Google ranking)
- Google ranks pages that are helpful, authoritative, and technically clean.
- Content must serve the reader first — optimization is secondary.
- Every page needs one clear topic, one primary keyword, clean structure.

### 2. AEO — Answer Engine Optimization
- First sentence under every H2 must directly answer the topic.
- Use definition format: "[Thing] is [definition]."
- FAQ section mandatory in every blog — minimum 5 questions.
- Each FAQ answer: 40–60 words, starts with direct answer.

### 3. GEO — Generative Engine Optimization
- Write authoritative statements — no hedging ("might", "could", "perhaps").
- Include stats with source: "70% of India internet traffic is mobile — Google India, 2024".
- Mention named entities clearly: "Naisora", "Bangalore", "restaurant website India".
- Add "About the Author" section on every blog.

### 4. E-E-A-T — Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness
- **Experience:** Show real-world context (Bangalore restaurants, Indian market data).
- **Expertise:** Author bio on every blog.
- **Authoritativeness:** External links to credible sources.
- **Trustworthiness:** Transparent pricing, honest comparisons.
